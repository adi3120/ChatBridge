About GGITS
Introduction:
Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Sciences is a professional institution in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. It was founded in 2003 and offers bachelor’s and master’s degrees, and diplomas in engineering, pharmacy, and management. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Praudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya and Institute has ME, EC, EE and CSE Accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) of the All India Council for Technical Education.
GGITS is part of the Gyan Ganga Group of Institutions

The Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Technical University, Bhopal.  The Institute has highest number of well-qualified & vastly experienced faculty members , State-of-the art fully Wi-fi enabled Infrastructure with a lush-green campus, one of its kind in the central region.
All the class-rooms are equipped with Smart Boards, multi-media set-up Fully air-conditioned Auditorium with a 600+ seating capacity. Exclusive tie-ups with multi-national companies and International Academies like Cisco Networking Academy, Oracle Academy, Microsoft Imagine Academy, AWS Academy LinkedIn Coaches and Centres of Excellence for IBM, Hexaware and Zensar Technologies for trainings on recent upcoming technologies.

Leaders in Placements in the Central region since last 06 Years Consecutively. 800 + Placements for passing out Batch 2021 and 130 students already placed in Reliance Jio from the Batch 2018-22. Major IT and core companies like TCS, Wipro Technologies, Tech. Mahindra, Infosys, Amdocs, Persistent, Reliance Jio, Essar Group, JMC Projects, ETA Engg.
